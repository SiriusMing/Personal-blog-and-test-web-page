What if I say I shall not wait!

Author:Emily Dickinson

What if I say I shall not wait!
What if I burst the fleshly Gate -
And pass Escaped - to thee!
What if I file this mortal - off -
See where it hurt me - That's enough -
And step in Liberty!
They cannot take me - any more!
Dungeons may call - and Guns implore -
Unmeaning - now - to me -
As laughter - was - an hour ago -
Or Laces - or a Travelling Show -
Or who died - yesterday!

My story

I love poetry because it is like a gentle spring, nourishing everything silently, evoking resonance in the soul, and enriching life with its beauty.

I love poetry because it's like a mirror, reflecting the emotions and thoughts deep within, allowing me to perceive the profound meaning of life.

Each poem is a dialogue of the soul, allowing me to feel the depth and breadth of life, guiding me through time and space, and exploring the innermost realms of the heart.
