My stories

A story with the song: Old Fashion

As the melody of "Old Fashion" by Back Number fills the room, a wave of nostalgia washes over me, carrying me back to simpler times.
I can't help but feel a pang of longing for the carefree days of my youth, when the biggest worry on my mind was completing my homework on time or deciding what to do with my friends on the weekends. Back then, the future seemed so distant, filled with endless possibilities waiting to be explored.
"Old Fashion" serves as a poignant reminder to cherish these moments, to hold onto them tightly as they slip through my fingers like grains of sand. For even as time marches relentlessly forward, these memories will remain etched in my heart, a testament to the beauty of youth and the fleeting nature of time.

The story of me and chocolate

When I was a child, chocolate was my greatest delight.
I remember saving up my allowance to buy my favorite bars and savoring each bite as if it were a precious treasure.
Every special occasion seemed to come with a box of chocolates, and the sweet taste always brought a smile to my face. Those moments are some of my fondest childhood memories.

More about chocolate and my childhood

I remember the way chocolate seemed to melt in my mouth, releasing its irresistible flavor and filling me with happiness. It was more than just a confection; it was a comfort, a reward, and a symbol of celebration.
From birthdays to holidays, chocolate was always present, bringing a touch of sweetness to every occasion. Whether it was a heart-shaped box of chocolates on Valentine's Day or a batch of homemade brownies on a rainy day, chocolate had a magical way of making even the ordinary moments feel special.

Brief Introduction of my favorite band: back number

Back Number is a Japanese pop rock band known for their heartfelt lyrics and memorable melodies. I first discovered their music during a challenging period in my life, and their songs resonated with me deeply.
Their music has a unique ability to capture the complexities of human emotions, and I found solace in their relatable lyrics and soulful compositions.
